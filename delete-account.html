Delete your Ripple account

This page applies to the Ripple app (package in.ripplesocial.app).

Delete your account in the app (immediate)

  1. Open Ripple and tap your profile photo (top-left of the Home tab)
  2. Tap Settings and privacy
  3. Scroll to Danger zone and tap Delete account
  4. Confirm the warning, then enter your password

Your account is deleted immediately and you are signed out.

Or request deletion by email

Email allenmurry0@gmail.com from the email address registered to your Ripple account with the subject "Ripple account deletion". We will delete the account within 30 days and confirm by email.

What is deleted, and what is kept

Deleted immediately: your profile, posts, comments, likes, stories, messages, follower/following relationships, notifications, and login sessions. Your account is no longer visible to any user and cannot be signed into.

Kept temporarily: in line with the Indian Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines) Rules, 2021, a sealed copy of the deleted account's registration information and content is retained for 180 days, solely for security purposes and to assist lawful investigations by authorised agencies. It is not used for any other purpose and is permanently and automatically erased after 180 days.

See the full Ripple privacy policy for more information.
