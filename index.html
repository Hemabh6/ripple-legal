Ripple — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Ripple ("the app", "we") is a social application offering a public feed, stories, and private messaging. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and the choices you have. By using Ripple you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: username, display name, email address, and a securely hashed password (we never store your password in plain text).
  • Content you create: posts, comments, stories, profile details, images you upload, likes, reposts, and follows.
  • Messages: chat messages and images you send are stored on our servers so they can be delivered and shown in your conversation history.
  • Reports: if you report content or another user, we store the report to review it.
  • Technical basics: standard connection logs (such as IP address and timestamps) needed to run and secure the service.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, or advertising identifiers.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate the service: showing feeds, delivering messages and notifications.
  • To keep the community safe: enforcing our rules, reviewing reports, and preventing abuse.
  • To secure accounts: authentication and session management.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not show third-party advertising.

3. Sharing

Content you post publicly (posts, stories, profile) is visible to other users. Private messages are visible only to conversation members. We share data with third parties only when required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. The chat section can additionally be protected with a PIN on your device.

5. Data retention and deleting your account

Your data is retained while your account is active. Stories are automatically deleted 24 hours after posting.

To delete your account and associated data, open the app and go to Settings and privacy → Danger zone → Delete account. After you confirm with your password, your account, posts, stories, messages, follows, and notifications are deleted immediately and permanently.

Alternatively, email allenmurry0@gmail.com from the email registered to your account and we will delete it within 30 days.

6. Children

Ripple is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Reporting and grievances

You can report posts and users in the app, and block any user. For grievances or questions about this policy, contact: allenmurry0@gmail.com.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be announced in the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
